About me

Welcome to my website! I am a Ph.D. student at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), Thuwal, Saudi Arabia. I received my B.S. degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2020, and M.S. degree from KAUST in 2022. My current research interests include stochastic geometry, satellite communications and wireless networks.
